❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼
To place an order: 1) Browse products and add them to cart 2) Click on cart icon 3) Review items and proceed to checkout 4) Choose payment method and complete payment 5) You'll receive order confirmation via SMS/Email.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept UPI payments through PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, and Amazon Pay. We also support credit/debit cards and net banking. All payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery (available in select cities) takes 1-2 business days. Free delivery is available on orders above ₹499.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused, in original packaging. Fashion items have a 15-day return policy. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
How do I cancel my order? ▼
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Go to 'My Orders', select the order and click 'Cancel Order'. Refunds for prepaid orders are processed within 3-5 business days.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. We never store your card details. All transactions are protected by advanced security measures.
